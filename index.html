Leading Through Innovation

The Bangladesh Institute of ICT in Development (BIID) Foundation, a social business, introduced the Nutrition Club (NC) platform back in 2015 to engage and benefit adolescents and youth in achieving healthy body and mind, with the skills to make informed decisions and lead healthy lifestyles.

Join Us Today

What is Nutrition Club?

Ignite the Spark of Young People!

The Nutrition Club provides a platform for adolescents and youth to build capacity, disseminate knowledge, and raise awareness about nutrition. Being part of the Nutrition Club is more than just nutrition and health - it's a lifestyle. Having the confidence and skills to lead healthy lifestyles not only improves overall well-being; it enables them to tackle life's challenges today and become leaders in their lives, careers, and communities as they grow into responsible adulthood.

ICT4D Strategy

Nutrition Club adopted ICT4D as a strategy to bring nutrition information, education and communication (IEC) more accessible & available through its e-Learning platform www.e-pushti.net.

Multistakeholder Partnership

Multistakeholder partnership approach made Nutrition Club more inclusive and BIID Foundation is constantly pursuing building collaboration with relevant government agencies, private sector, academic institutions, and development partners.

Leadership Development

Nutrition Club fosters leadership, networking, and skill-building opportunities for the adolescents & youth to explore their potential towards resolving social issues, focusing on malnutrition.

Nourishing the Future

Nutrition Club is mandated to build leadership skills and create opportunities for young people to improve nutrition for all. NC envision to create a well nourished society to support national and global efforts to end malnutrition across Bangladesh, and globally.

Nutrition Club Programs Engage Youth to Lead in Nutrition

Nutrition Club programs are designed targeting adolescents and youths to learn by doing. Members engaged in a wide range of projects focusing on nutrition which covers health, hygiene, agriculture, skill development, creative works, environment, and civic engagement under the direct guidance of the Mentors at respective institutions.

1. Building Awareness, Networking & Engaging

Creating connections and spreading awareness about nutrition and healthy lifestyles in communities.

2. Developing Team Building, Leadership & Soft Skills

Fostering leadership qualities and essential soft skills through various training programs and workshops.

3. Dissemination of Nutrition Knowledge

Sharing vital nutrition information through various channels and platforms to reach wider audiences.

4. Organize and Participate in National & International Events

Active participation in significant events to promote nutrition awareness on national and global levels.

Making ICT Works for Nutrition

e-Pushti

An online learning platform (www.e-pushti.net) has been developed and designed to create opportunities for adolescents and young people to learn about nutrition, healthy lifestyles, and various social issues that directly and indirectly affect physical and mental health, and raise awareness.

Click to Join www.e-pushti.net

What e-Pushti Offers

  • Online courses
  • Library (information and educational materials, national policies, guidelines)
  • Webinars/topic-based discussions and training
  • Vlogs (videos)
  • Nutrition club information (Concept Note, maps, membership forms)
  • Hosting online discussions with partners

Why Choose e-Pushti?

  • Free online education
  • Gain the knowledge and skills needed for a healthy lifestyle
  • Support in leadership development
  • Easy participation through digital labs and mobile phones
