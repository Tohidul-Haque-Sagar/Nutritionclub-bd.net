About Us

The Bangladesh Institute of ICT in Development (BIID) Foundation, a social business, introduced the Nutrition Club (NC) platform back in 2015 to engage and benefit adolescents and youth in achieving healthy body and mind, with the skills to make informed decisions and lead healthy lifestyles.

What is Nutrition Club?

Ignite the Spark of Young People!

The Nutrition Club provides a platform for adolescents and youth to build capacity, disseminate knowledge, and raise awareness about nutrition. Being part of the Nutrition Club is more than just nutrition and health - it's a lifestyle.

Having the confidence and skills to lead healthy lifestyles not only improves overall well-being; it enables them to tackle life's challenges today and become leaders in their lives, careers, and communities as they grow into responsible adulthood.
